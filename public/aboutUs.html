SaveURSpace Overview

SaveURSpace is a reservation system designed to help users efficiently book collaborative spaces for group projects, meetings, or study sessions. The system allows users to easily select and reserve available rooms based on their preferred time slots. These rooms are equipped with various tools and technologies to enhance group work and foster productive learning environments. Whether for collaborative study or a team project, SaveURSpace ensures you have access to the perfect space for your needs.

LIBRARY MISSION

The QCU libraries, as part of the academic community, are committed to providing equal access to library collections, services, and facilities that shall assist the academe in their quest for knowledge, and yet accessible to all.

LIBRARY VISION

The university libraries are engaged in learning and discovery as essential participants in access to and preservation of materials to meet the needs of present and future generations of students. We explore and implement innovative technologies and services to deliver information conveniently to library clientele, anytime/anyplace.
