Collaborative Room Guidelines

  1. Reservations must be made at least one (1) day in advance.
  2. Maximum reservation time is two (2) hours per group/reservation daily. Extension will be subject to room availability.
  3. Please ensure that the room is clean and organized after use. Any damage or loss of equipment should be reported immediately.
  4. Food and drinks are not allowed inside the collaborative rooms.
  5. Be considerate of other users. Keep noise levels down.
