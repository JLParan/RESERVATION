For Collab Room Reservation please READ the guidelines.

1. Collaboration rooms may be reserved one day in advance. Same-day reservations are automatically cancelled.

2. If a group does not claim a reservation within 15 minutes of the scheduled time, it will be forfeited.

3. Collab Rooms may be reserved for a maximum of 1 hour per day.

4. Each Collab Room's capacity is a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 10 persons.

5. Eating and drinking are not allowed inside the Collab Room.
